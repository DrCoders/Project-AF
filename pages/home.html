Preliminary Care Unit

  • Preliminary Care Unit(PCU) is so named because patient is first admitted to and receive first medical care
  • PCU unit is an special unit which differ from the usual ward in a hospital
  • This is the place where doctors are decided to whether patient is going to get discharged or admitted to a ward in order to more medical treatments
  • There are many functions, from the patient part starting from patient registration to discharge or transfer to another ward
  • There is a registration for patient when patient admit to PCU
  • But also there are another separate registration to provide admission number
  • In PCU there is a PCU sheet for each and every patient
  • It will record all patient details ,admission date time ,admitted medical condition, doctors examined date and time, doctor's name, assigned medical drug, medical test and their result
